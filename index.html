Two ways
to feel like
Palm Springs.

Two distinct directions for The Sandwich Spot — Palm Springs. Both lean into the comedy of the menu names and Palm Springs identity. Both put Dutch Crunch front and center. Both look nothing like the franchise template. They differ in register: A is comedy-club loud, B is supper-club refined-but-cheeky.

— pick whichever makes you laugh first.

— quick reference —

A vs B

A · EMERGENCY
  • Loud, comedic, urgent.
  • Punk-zine + 911 dispatch + diner.
  • Tomato red on mayo cream.
  • Anton screams. Caveat scribbles.
  • Doodles front-and-center.
B · POSTCARD
  • Refined, editorial, quietly cheeky.
  • Travel magazine + supper club.
  • Terracotta on sun-bleached cream.
  • Cormorant tells stories. Caveat whispers.
  • Doodles in the margins.

A reader landing on A laughs first. A reader landing on B feels the place's history first, then catches the joke.
