## Flagged for Dena's confirmation

These are decisions made or details written that should be confirmed before any client-facing use:

1. **"Dena & the crew"** signature line on Direction A's mid-page strip and **"Operated by Corlyn & Dena"** in both footers. We named Dena and Corlyn explicitly. Confirm both are comfortable being credited; consider whether Corlyn (current owner) should be primary, Dena as catering/contact.
2. **Reference to the previous owner is not made on either page.** This was an intentional design choice — to keep the tone celebratory and forward-looking — but if Dena would like a discreet "in memory of" line we can add it (suggested placement: small italic line in the editorial Direction B intro).
3. **Direction A "Doctor Dena" character** uses Dena's first name as a recurring comedic persona who annotates the menu. This is the tightest comedic device in Direction A. Confirm Dena enjoys this — she's the protagonist of the joke.
4. **"For Frank, of course."** "Bob Hope. Don't argue with the legacy." "Manilow's hottest spot." "Suzanne. We squeeze hard." "I got you babe." — sandwich captions written in a familiar voice referencing each celebrity. Confirm the tone is right.
5. **"Wintered in Rancho Mirage. Drank martinis at Melvyn's."** — Hall of Fame entry for Sinatra references the Melvyn's Restaurant at the Ingleside Inn (where Sinatra famously drank). Verified historically; flagging because we're putting words in Frank's mouth.
6. **"Built a UFO-shaped house in the hills you can still see from the freeway."** — refers to Bob Hope's Lautner-designed house. Verified. Flagging because it's a specific local reference and we want Dena's blessing.
7. **"Lives here. Yes, still. Yes, the song. Yes, hot."** — Manilow Hall of Fame entry. Verified Manilow lives in Palm Springs. Tone is the question.
8. **Direction A "Severe Hangover" CASE 02 references "Saguaro pool / Bootlegger Tiki"** as Palm Springs nightlife landmarks. Verified both exist (Saguaro Hotel + Bootlegger Tiki bar). Confirm tone is right.
9. **Tagline phrasing** — we used "Love All ♥ Feed All" verbatim from the franchise. Confirm the Palm Springs branch is comfortable continuing to use the franchise tagline.
10. **The Dirty Weekend / The DILF / The MILF / But It's a Dry Heat / Frowzy Red Head** — all kept on the website at full visibility because they're the brand's voice. If Dena wants any of these dimmed/hidden on the website (vs. the in-store menu), flag and we'll adjust.
